Spacing & Layout

Spacing follows a base-4 scale. Common values used across components:

/* Base-4 spacing scale */
4px    — inner padding, tight gaps
8px    — compact element spacing
12px   — standard gap between related items
16px   — card padding, section gaps
20px   — generous padding
24px   — major section spacing
32px   — page-level margins
40px   — hero/section padding
48px   — major section breaks
