Design Principles

Glassmorphism

Layered translucency with backdrop blur. Depth through light, not heavy shadows.

Token-driven

Every color, radius, and shadow flows from design tokens. One change propagates everywhere.

Theme-aware

Light and dark adapt via semantic tokens. Components need zero theme-specific code.

Accessible

WCAG 2.1 AA contrast. Full keyboard navigation. Reduced-motion support.
